GPR-aided localization for construction and survey robots operating in
visually degraded, GNSS-denied environments. The pipeline uses synchronized
wheel encoder, IMU, GPR, and total-station data from the public
[CMU-GPR dataset](https://github.com/cmubig/CMU-GPR-Dataset).
